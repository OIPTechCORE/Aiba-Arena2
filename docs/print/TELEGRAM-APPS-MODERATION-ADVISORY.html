Telegram Apps Moderation Advisory — AIBA Arena

This advisory supports registering AIBA Arena in the Telegram Apps Moderation Center for listing on tapps.center and exposure via @tapps_bot.

A) Probability of Approval

Overall estimate: Medium–High (≈65–80%) if policy-compliant and well presented

Strengths

  1. TON integration — Wallet connect (TonConnect), TON blockchain (AIBA jettons, NEUR credits).
  2. Clear value proposition — Play-to-earn broker battles, referral system, guilds, marketplace.
  3. No obvious gambling — Battles are skill/simulation-based, not pure chance.
  4. User onboarding — Clear flow (connect wallet, create broker, run battles, earn rewards).

Risk factors

  1. - Clear disclosure (no false promises).
  2. - No misleading “guaranteed profit” language.
  3. Referral rewards — Tiered bonuses (10×, 100×) are common; keep them transparent.
  4. Wallet requirement — Referee must connect wallet to apply a referral; moderators may question if this feels too restrictive.

Practical tips

  • Use high-quality app icon and screenshots.
  • Have a privacy policy and terms of service ready.
  • Ensure the app works smoothly on first launch (no critical bugs).
