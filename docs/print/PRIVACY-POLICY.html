Privacy Policy — AIBA Arena

Last updated: See document date or changelog.

AIBA Arena ("we", "our", "the app") is a Telegram Mini App for AI broker battles, play-to-earn mechanics, and community features. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 From Telegram

When you launch AIBA Arena through Telegram:

  • Username — Used for leaderboards, referrals, and display
  • First name, last name — Optional; used for personalization and display
  • Language code — Used to tailor content where supported
  • Profile photo URL — Optional; used for avatar display if available

1.2 From Wallet Connection

If you connect a TON wallet (TonConnect):

  • We do not store private keys. Wallet operations are performed through TonConnect in your device.

1.3 Gameplay & Usage Data

  • Leaderboard rankings — Your scores, AIBA, NEUR, and battle counts may be publicly visible
  • Referral codes and usage — To credit referrers and referees
  • Guild membership, staking, market activity — For in-game features

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Display leaderboards and community rankings
  • Process referrals and Creator Economy payouts
  • Support marketplace, P2P, and donation flows
  • Detect fraud, anomalies, and abuse (see Moderation)
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data
  • Telegram Init Data is verified cryptographically to prevent spoofing

4. Data Retention

  • You may request deletion of your data subject to applicable law and our ability to fulfill the request

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us through the support channels in the app.

6. Third Parties

  • TonConnect / TON — Wallet connection and blockchain transactions are handled by TonConnect and the TON network.
  • Hosting — The app may be deployed on platforms such as Vercel or similar.

7. Children

AIBA Arena is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via the app or in-product notice. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions: use the support options in the app (Settings → Support) or the project’s official channels.
